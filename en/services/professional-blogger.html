It is easy to make something difficult, it is difficult to make something easy.

Writing about technology is a good way to showcase your expertise and attract attention. However, writing a coherent and catchy story can be a challenge for techies. Your sales and marketing department is better at this, but does not have the technical expertise to put the real details on paper. An article about IT that does not adequately describe the technical details, will make your organization look incompetent.

The solution is simple: let me guide you in writing the article. As a blogger and a scientist I have written prize-winning texts. I tailor the content to your audience and translate the technical details in such a way that people without an IT-background understand the subject matter.
